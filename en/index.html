Studio Only is €125 for 2 hours, €245 for 4 hours and €475 for a full day (8 hours). The equipment add-on with professional lighting starts at €35. All prices net, equipment not included.

The studio price includes use of the infinity wall, the kitchenette, the make-up area, the changing room and Wi-Fi. Professional lighting and further props can be flexibly added as an equipment add-on.

Our "equipment add-on" includes professional lighting such as softboxes and flash systems. We're happy to send you a detailed list of available equipment on request.

Yes – for multi-day bookings or regular use we're glad to put together an individual offer. Just get in touch.

Yes, free parking is available directly in front of the studio. For larger productions with several vehicles we ask for a brief arrangement in advance.

Simply send us a request via the booking form with your preferred date. We check availability and get back to you within 24 hours. Once you confirm, the slot is reserved for you and you'll receive all further information.

If your shoot runs longer than planned, an extension is possible spontaneously, provided the studio is free afterwards. Each additional hour started is charged at the regular hourly rate. We recommend planning a sufficient time buffer from the start.

For cancellations up to 14 days before the booked date, 50 % of the rental cost applies. For cancellations within 7 days of the date we unfortunately have to charge 100 % of the cost.
